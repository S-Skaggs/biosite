AJ's Beginning

AJ grew up around the Cuyahoga Falls area in Ohio.

He attended k-12 with a focus on college preparatory courses. Most of his teenage years were spent rebuilding engines and racing cars. In fact his idea of a fun Saturday was to pull the engine from a friend's car and work on it.
