AJ signed up for the Army enlisted reserve in December of 1991. He departed for Basic Training at Fort Sill, OK the following spring. After graduation he shipped out for advanced individual training at Aberdeen Proving Ground at Edgewood Arsenal, MD.

His Military Occupational Specialty (MOS) was 63W, Wheeled Vehicle Mechanic. He went on to serve eight years active reserve as part of the 682d Heavy Equipment Maintenance Company (HEMCO) and then 758th HEMCO, out of Ravenna OH and Canton OH respectively.

AJ's primary service role was in component rebuild for High Mobility Multipurpose Wheeled Vehicle (HMMWV) and Heavy Expanded Mobility Tactical Truck (HEMTT) trucks, along with Cummins diesel engines and powertrains for 2.5-ton and 5-ton utility military vehicles.

He has been to Germany, Austria, Italy and the Republic of Panama across his military service career.
